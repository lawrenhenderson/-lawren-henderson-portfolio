About

Hi, I'm Lawren.

I started my career producing unscripted television. Duck Dynasty, Shark Week, Celebrity Wife Swap. The work was exciting: craft a story that demanded attention. In 2016, MarketScale pinged me on LinkedIn and pulled me into content marketing. I never looked back.

These days I specialize in the stories other people can't figure out how to tell. The ones buried inside complex technology, clinical research, or a product that's genuinely hard to explain. I've written for cybersecurity firms, ERP platforms, telehealth companies, and YouTube itself. I've also written a feature-length screenplay that went on to be independently produced.

I made the move from Los Angeles to San Juan, Puerto Rico. I wanted a new adventure, and San Juan called like a siren. When I'm not writing I'm traveling, sometimes solo, sometimes with friends, always looking for the kind of experience that sharpens me into a better storyteller.
