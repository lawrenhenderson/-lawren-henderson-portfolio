About

Hi, I'm Lawren.

Welcome! I started my career scripting and producing reality television. Duck Dynasty, Shark Week, Celebrity Wife Swap. In 2016, content agency MarketScale pinged me on LinkedIn and pulled me into marketing. I've been loving it ever since.

These days, I specialize in the stories others struggle to tell, whether that's a sophisticated technology or a brand trying to make a name for itself. I've written for cybersecurity firms, ERP platforms, telehealth companies, YouTube (Google), and much more. I've also written a movie that was independently produced and is available to stream.

In 2021, I moved to Puerto Rico in search of a new adventure. When I'm not writing I'm traveling, sometimes solo, sometimes with friends, always looking for the kind of experience that makes me a better storyteller. Click around my portfolio to explore my work.
